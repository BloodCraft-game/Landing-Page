CHOOSE YOUR PATH

Two ancient factions wage eternal war. Each offers unique abilities, strategies, and paths to power. Your choice will define your destiny in the blood-soaked lands.

Cult of the Damned

"Steal. Curse. Rule."

Masters of dark rituals and forbidden magic. The Cult excels at resource manipulation, draining enemies while empowering allies. Perfect for strategists who prefer cunning over brute force.

  • Shadow Theft: Steal resources from weaker opponents
  • Curse Mastery: Debuff enemies for 3 turns
  • Blood Pact: Sacrifice HP for powerful spells
  • Necromancy: Summon fallen warriors

Sect of the Possessed

"Counter. Rage. Break."

Berserker warriors channeling demonic fury. The Sect dominates through raw power and devastating counterattacks. Ideal for aggressive players who thrive in direct combat.

  • Berserker Rage: +50% damage when below 30% HP
  • Demon Armor: Reflect 25% of damage taken
  • Chain Breaker: Immune to all crowd control
  • Blood Frenzy: Each kill grants stacking buffs

THE CYCLE OF BLOOD

Master three interconnected systems to dominate the battlefield. Every action feeds into the next, creating a brutal economy of violence and power.

BUILD

Fortify your domain with cursed structures and dark defenses

Construct Blood Altars, Obsidian Towers, and Ritual Chambers. Each building generates passive resources and unlocks powerful abilities. Upgrade your stronghold to withstand enemy sieges.

HUNT

Feed on the blighted woods and corrupt dungeons

Venture into procedurally generated PvE zones. Slay corrupted beasts, loot legendary artifacts, and gather Blood Essence. Higher risk zones yield greater rewards but attract other players.

STEAL

Take from the weak, dominate the strong

Launch raids on enemy territories. Plunder resources, capture prisoners, and destroy rival structures. Successful attacks earn you reputation and unlock exclusive NFT trophies. Defend or be destroyed.
